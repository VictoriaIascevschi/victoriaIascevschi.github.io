The Importance of Planning and Organising

Learning outcome

You can substantiate the importance of planning and organising your study (and work) and have indicated the extent to which planning is a development theme for you.

Question 1: Why do you think planning is important during your study programme? Include the terms effective and efficient in your answer.

To my mind, planning is crucial during my study program because it helps me manage my time correctly to fulfill all tasks properly before the deadline. While organizing my time I can put priorities this means: I focus on the most important tasks first to ensure my efforts lead to a successful result in a more effective way. This way, it saves my time and energy wisely to achieve success more easily. As a result, I am improving my efficiency in studying. In addition, a well-organized study plan means that I will understand and absorb the course material and have a sense of control over my studies. Also, it reduces stress as all assignments are done on time as it sometimes impacts negatively on academic success.

Question 2: To what extent should or do you want to develop your planning skills further? Substantiate your answer with at least one example.

I would like to not only improve my effectiveness but also my efficiency. I want to manage to do complex tasks quickly just in a few specific steps without wasting time. That is why I want to enhance my abilities in planning. I guess this is very useful especially while working on long-term projects because planning helps to ensure that the work is made on time and it was done before the deadline. What is more, the ability to manage time is crucial for project leaders and team members as it is easier to fulfill all responsibilities.
